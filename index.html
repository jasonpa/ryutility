Useful, free web tools

A small, growing collection of tools with no sign-up and no clutter.

Ryutility builds calculators for runners who train by numbers — VDOT training zones, heart-rate zones, race pace, race prediction, split strategy, and treadmill conversion, all using established, published methods rather than guesswork. Every calculator is paired with a guide that explains the reasoning behind the numbers, not just the numbers themselves.
